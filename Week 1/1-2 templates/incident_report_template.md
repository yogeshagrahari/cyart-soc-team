## Recommendations

1. Implement account lockout policy (lock after 5 failed attempts in 10 minutes).
2. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all privileged accounts.
3. Restrict direct network logons for built-in Administrator account.
4. Audit all recently installed services and remove unauthorized entries.
5. Deploy application whitelisting to prevent unauthorized executables.
6. Review and update incident response runbook with lessons learned.
